5-Day Maximum Dunk Plan

This plan combines a 1-hour hoop session and a 15-min room session each day to maximize your vertical jump in 5 days. Follow the steps carefully and focus on explosiveness + mechanics.

Daytime Hoop Session (1 Hour)

1️⃣ Warm-Up (10 min)

  • Jog or march in place – 3 min
  • Leg swings (forward/backward & side) – 2 min
  • High knees – 1 min
  • Butt kicks – 1 min
  • Hip circles – 1 min
  • Arm swings – 2 min

2️⃣ Approach + Jump Mechanics (15 min)

  • Stand 6–8 steps from the hoop.
  • Run toward hoop:
    • Long step → short, quick last step (plant)
    • Quick dip → explode upward (triple extension: ankles, knees, hips)
    • Swing arms fully → touch rim or dunk
  • 10 jumps in a row → rest 60 sec. Repeat 4 sets.

3️⃣ Explosive Leg Jumps (15 min)

  • Squat Jumps – 10 reps × 3 sets (rest 30–45 sec)
  • Two-Foot Explosive Jumps – 8–10 reps × 3 sets (rest 30–45 sec)
  • Calf Raise + Jump – 15 reps × 2 sets (rest 30 sec)

4️⃣ Rim Practice / Dunk Attempts (15 min)

  • Approach like in step 2 → two-hand dunk or rim touch
  • 12–15 reps in a row → rest 45–60 sec. Repeat 3 sets.

5️⃣ Cool Down / Stretch (5 min)

  • Light walking – 2 min
  • Stretch calves, quads, hamstrings, hips – 3 min
Tip: Sleep 8–9 hours, eat protein, and swing arms fully on every jump. Visualize your approach + dip + jump motion each time.

Evening Room Session (15 min at 8 PM)

1️⃣ Quick Warm-Up (2 min)

  • Jog in place or high knees – 1 min
  • Arm circles & leg swings – 1 min

2️⃣ Explosive Jumps (6 min)

  • Squat Jumps – 10 reps × 2 sets (rest 30 sec)
  • Two-Foot Explosive Jumps – 8–10 reps × 2 sets (rest 30 sec)
  • Calf Raise + Jump – 12–15 reps × 1 set (rest 30 sec)

3️⃣ Approach Simulation Drill (5 min)

  • Mimic your hoop approach in your room:
  • 3–4 steps forward → plant both feet → quick dip → jump straight up → pretend to grab rim or dunk
  • 10 reps × 3 sets (rest 30 sec between sets)

4️⃣ Cool Down (2 min)

  • Light marching in place – 1 min
  • Stretch calves, quads, hamstrings – 1 min
Tip: Focus on fast dip → triple extension → arm swing. Even without a hoop, this reinforces your dunk pattern and explosiveness.
